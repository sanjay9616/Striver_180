| Algorithm              | Time Complexity          | Space Complexity  |
|----------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------- |
| Brute Force            | O(N * log(N))            | O(N)              |

### Approach 2: Using Max-Heap

| Algorithm              | Time Complexity          | Space Complexity  |
|----------------------- | ------------------------ | ----------------- |
| Max-heap               | O(K log D + D log D)     | O(D)              |

where D = Count of distinct element
